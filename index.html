Finnlee & Falynn Chore Tracker

Each kid can earn up to $100 base pay, plus optional bonuses. The money is weighted by chore difficulty. Hard chores are worth more. Easier chores are worth less.

IMPORTANT: If Dad checks a chore and it was rushed, sloppy, half-done, or not completed the right way, the money for that chore gets decreased until it is fixed correctly.

Weighted Pay

Hardest: garage and breezeway
Next: bedrooms
Next: clothing and outdoor work
Easy: everything else

Status Rules

Each chore can be marked Not Started or Done. Dad can also mark a chore as Failed Dad Check, which cuts that chore’s payout in half until it is corrected.

Saved Automatically

Selections are saved in this browser using localStorage. Refreshing the page will keep the progress and payout totals on this device.
