Finnlee & Falynn Chore Tracker

Each kid can earn $100, but the money is weighted across the chores. Harder chores are worth more money. Easier chores are worth less money. The lists are side by side so Dad can track both at the same time.

IMPORTANT: If Dad checks a chore and it was rushed, sloppy, or not actually done the right way, the money for that chore goes DOWN until it is fixed.

Weighted Pay

Hardest: garage and breezeway
Next: bedrooms
Next: clothing and outdoor work
Easy: everything else

Status Rules

Mark each chore as Not Started, Done, or Failed Dad Check. A failed check cuts the chore payout in half until it is corrected.

Bonus Rules

Bonuses can be added for meaningful helping, not rushing, and great teamwork.
